Rock, Paper, Scissors

In this game, you will chose between rock, paper, or scissors, in a high stakes action-packed game where anything can happen.

The winner of the game is based on what you choose.

  • If you pick rock, and the computer picks paper, you lose!
  • If you pick rock, and the computer picks scissors, you win!
  • If you pick paper, and the computer picks scissors, you lose!
  • If you pick paper, and the computer picks rock, you win!
  • If you pick scissors, and the computer picks rock, you lose!
  • If you pick scissors, and the computer picks paper, you win!
